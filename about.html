History of Japan

Brief history of Japan, The main or most famous parts of japanese history.

Heian period

Heian period was between 794 and 1185.

Heian (平安) means "Peace" in japanese. The Heian period is considered the peak of the Japanese imperial court, noted for its art, especially poetry and literature.

You may know The Tale of Genji novel by Murasaki Shikibu, Which was written at that time period and it's also considered to be one of the world's first novels.

Sengoku period

Sengoku period which mean Warring States period. Was the period in Japanese history with civil wars and social upheaveals.

The Popular TV series Shōgun (2024) is set on sengoku period. It won 18 Emmy awards

Edo period

Edo period was between 1603 until 1868. When the Tokugawa family ruled Japan.

Edo period the final period of traditional Japan, a time of internal peace, art, political stability and economic growth.

Meiji period

Meiji period was between 1868-1912 and it is the era of modernization and Westernization of Japan.
